Cruises to Antarctica

The magical world of icebergs and penguins

Journey to the ends of the earth

This magical journey will help you get to know the amazing world of Antarctica. Our cruise ship will take you to the ends of the earth, where you can see: icebergs, several types of penguins, whales, fur seals and much more.

Advantages

Small cruise ship
With a capacity of just 300 guests, a cruise ship will help you avoid noise and queues and give you the opportunity to enjoy the views and nature.
All inclusive system
All of our cruises are All Inclusive. Great food and an unlimited bar are available for you 24 hours.
Excursion tours on Zodiac
Excursion tours to the shores of Antarctica are made on equipped boats of the Zodiac type, accompanied by experienced guides.
